Our Promise

United by our passion for food and family, we bring you only the very best that the fields and waterways of Ireland have to offer, directly to your table. Our local suppliers are an essential part of The Nosh & Co family and ensure that your meal is only one step away from its roots. Nothing goes together better than great food and great company, and from our family to yours, we promise you food that satisfies both body and soul.
